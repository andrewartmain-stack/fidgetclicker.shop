Shipping Information

We want to get your FidgetClicker to you as quickly as possible! Here's everything you need to know about our shipping process.

Shipping Costs

Free Shipping: Orders of 3 or more items qualify for free shipping!

Standard Shipping: For orders with fewer than 3 items, standard shipping fees apply and will be calculated at checkout.

Delivery Time

Once your order is processed and shipped, delivery typically takes:

  • 2-9 business days depending on your location
  • Orders are usually processed and shipped within 1-2 business days
  • You will receive a tracking number via email once your order ships

Tracking Your Order

After your order ships, you'll receive an email with:

  • Your tracking number
  • A direct link to track your package
  • Estimated delivery date

You can also track your order anytime by visiting our Track Order page.

Shipping Locations

We currently ship to most locations worldwide. If you have questions about shipping to your specific location, please contact us.

Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days. During high-volume periods (holidays, sales events), processing may take slightly longer.

Order Modifications

If you need to modify your order, please contact us at support@fidgetclicker.shop as soon as possible. Once an order has been shipped, we cannot make changes.

Questions?

If you have any questions about shipping or your order, please don't hesitate to reach out to our customer support team at support@fidgetclicker.shop.
